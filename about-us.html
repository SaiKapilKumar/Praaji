Our Story

At Praaji Strictions LLP, we are more than just an interior design company; we are storytellers of space. Our journey began with a shared passion for transforming ordinary environments into extraordinary experiences.

Inspired by the diverse personalities and unique styles of our clients, we embarked on a mission to create bespoke interior designs that resonate deeply with individual narratives. With a keen eye for detail and a commitment to excellence, we carefully curate every aspect of our designs to reflect the essence of each client's vision. From residential sanctuaries to vibrant commercial hubs and welcoming hospitality spaces, we infuse every project with creativity, innovation, and dedication.Our team of skilled professionals brings together expertise from various disciplines, ensuring that every design is smartly executed to surpass expectations.
